Create - Build - Explore

We at The Builders Shed offer a variety of services from talks, tutorials and private lesons on a variety of subjects
to access to electrical tools such as oscilloscopes, signal generators, power supplies and soldering irons as well as access to computers and raspberry PI's.

Our trained staff will be avaliblal when ever our door are open.

Eqipment rental and pricing

We understand that not everyone knows what you need and where to start
when it comes to starting your journey into electronics or programming.
So we offer a variaty of electronic and computer equipment for rental.

Each workbench comes with a standard tool kit of screw drivers, wire stripers, pliers and various other basic tools.

Tools and equipment for rental

Standard work bench

The standard workbench consists of a bench and seating area large enough for two people with access to power outlets and WiFi.
This would be ideal for anyone wishing to bring thire own computer or tools,
Larger benches and seating areas can be arranged but need to be booked before hand with the price adjusted acordingly

Cost per session £15

Electronics workbench

The Electroniocs workbench is the same as the standard workbench but with access to electronics tools and testing equipment.
-Power supply
-Soldering iron
-Multi-meter
-Oscilloscope
-Signal generators and more.

Cost per session £25

Computer work station

The computer work station is like the standard workbench with a seating area large enough for two people and access to power outlets and WiFi.
Each computer work station consists of a AWOW Mini Desktop Computer with an Intel Celeron J4115 and 8GB running either Windows 10 or Ubuntu.
